Our Menu

Stockholms Mat is a progression of rare and beautiful ingredients where texture, flavour
and harmony is paramount.

Continually inspired by nature, the creative process for chef Nazzarato
begins in working closely with the farmers, fishermen, producers and artisans
who cultivate bespoke produce exclusively for Stockholms Mat. For each dish, the growth
of every element and the selection of every ceramic piece is carefully
considered and crafted for its role in the dining experience.

Stockholms Mat brings a personal, interactive passage for diners through Nazzarato’s
culinary evolution.
